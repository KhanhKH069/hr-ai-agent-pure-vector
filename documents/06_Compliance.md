## Labor Law (10 câu)

### Q1: Employment contract
**Câu hỏi:** Hợp đồng lao động như thế nào?
**Biến thể:**
- Contract type
- Employment agreement

**Trả lời:**
- Probation: 2-3 months
- Fixed-term: 1-3 years
- Indefinite: After 2 fixed-term contracts
- Governed by: Vietnam Labor Code 2019

---

### Q2: Probation terms
**Câu hỏi:** Thử việc bao lâu?
**Biến thể:**
- Trial period
- Probation duration

**Trả lời:**
- Entry level: 30 days
- Mid-level: 60 days
- Senior: 90 days (max)
- Salary: 85% during probation

### Q5: Termination conditions
**Câu hỏi:** Khi nào có thể sa thải?
**Biến thể:**
- Dismissal grounds
- Firing conditions

**Trả lời:**
Legal grounds:
- Serious misconduct
- Repeated violations
- Incompetence after training
- Company restructure
- Economic reasons
Must follow due process

---

### Q6: Severance pay
**Câu hỏi:** Thôi việc có trợ cấp không?
**Biến thể:**
- Termination pay
- Separation benefits

**Trả lời:**
Per Labor Code:
- 0.5 month salary per year worked
- Minimum 1 month notice
- Unused leave: Paid
- Resignation: No severance (unless negotiated)

---

### Q7: Labor union
**Câu hỏi:** Có công đoàn không?
**Biến thể:**
- Trade union
- Workers organization

**Trả lời:**
- Company has union
- Voluntary membership
- Monthly fee: 1% salary
- Benefits: Legal support, welfare
- Representative elected annually

### Q8: Workplace safety
**Câu hỏi:** Luật an toàn lao động?
**Biến thể:**
- OSH regulations
- Safety compliance

**Trả lời:**
Company complies:
- Annual safety training (mandatory)
- Fire drills: Quarterly
- First aid trained staff
- Accident reporting: Within 24h
- PPE provided where needed

### Q9: Harassment policy

- Workplace harassment

Zero tolerance:
- Sexual harassment: Immediate termination

- Report to: HR or hotline 1900-xxx

### Q10: Discrimination policy
**Câu hỏi:** Có phân biệt đối xử không?
